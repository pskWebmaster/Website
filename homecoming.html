Homecoming 2020

In order to ensure the health and safety of the community, the Georgia Tech Alumni Association has cancelled all in person events for Homecoming 2020.  Phi Sigma Kappa will not be hosting an in-person gathering.  

The Kappa Deuteron Alumni Association will be awarding its annual active member scholarships over a WebEx broadcast at a date that is still to be determined.  An email notification of the finalized date and time will be sent to those on our alunmi communications list.  If you have not been receiving the most recent newsletters and would like to, please email psk.kd.alumni.communications@gmail.com to be added.  Please check back here or await notification so you can tune in to help recognize the hard work of our active chapter during these challenging times. 

We hope everyone is staying safe and healthy and look forward to being able to gather in person for future homecoming celebrations.  Go Jackets!
